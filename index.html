About

Hello! My name is Linda and I paint plant pots inspired by native and naturalized organisms in the PNW region, local scenery from hikes and nearby neighborhoods, and organic forms and color palettes from the natural world. The goal is to fuel my plant hoarding by selling some of my art. Pieces are made with acrylic and ink on terracotta plant pots, and then coated with polyurethane/liquitex varnish for waterproofing/weather protection.

When I'm not painting or gardening (or working my day job), I'm either attending events with NW Veg and Portland Metro Veggies, keeping up with the Oregon Mycological Society, walking pups, playing guitar, hiking, or spending time with friends. I love finding new plant-based eateries and spotting fungi and wildflowers on walks. Thank you for visiting my site!

Commission Info

I charge for the consultation/ideation process, availability of pot(s), the size of the pot(s), and shipping. Pot sizes will also depend on availability, but range generally between 2" to 5.5", unless you would like a larger size (and in that case we can try to work something out). My pricing model is still under general revision, but would love to work for you to bring home your next new pot :)

1. Consultation/drafts — $10+

I'll send a draft and up to 2 revisions to your pot design. The base draft fee is $10, and each revision is $5-10 depending on the complexity.

2. Pot sizes — $15+

2" pots — $15

3.5"-4" pots — $35-$60 depending on complexity

4.5" pots — $60-80 depending on complexity

5.5" pots — $80-120 depending on complexity

3. Shipping — $10

Shipping has a flat fee of $10. Unfortunately I only ship within the USA. Please reach out if you have any unanswered questions!
